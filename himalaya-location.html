Himalaya

The Himalayas, a magnificent mountain range stretching across northern India, is home to the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest and Kanchenjunga. Known for its stunning landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, and rich cultural heritage, the Himalayas offer an unparalleled experience for adventurers, spiritual seekers, and nature lovers.

Highlights

The Himalayas offer a unique blend of adventure, culture, and spirituality. From towering peaks to sacred monasteries, lush valleys to glacial rivers, the region provides a serene backdrop for trekking, meditation, and exploration. The Himalayas are also known for their biodiversity, encompassing several national parks and wildlife sanctuaries.

  • Trek to Everest Base Camp or Annapurna Circuit
  • Visit sacred monasteries like Hemis and Thiksey
  • Explore glacial rivers and high-altitude lakes
  • Trek through lush valleys and glacial terrain

Activities

The Himalayas provide a range of activities for visitors, from trekking to the base of Mount Everest to meditative retreats in remote monasteries. Adventure enthusiasts can engage in white-water rafting, paragliding, and mountain biking, while others may opt for serene walks, wildlife safaris, or yoga and meditation amid nature's beauty.

  • Trek to Annapurna, Everest Base Camp, and other trails
  • Paragliding in the Solang Valley
  • Camp under the stars in the Himalayas
  • Join yoga and meditation retreats

Accommodation

Stay amidst the majestic Himalayas in various accommodations, from luxury hotels to simple homestays. Choose to stay in eco-friendly lodges with panoramic views of the mountains, where you can enjoy the serene environment, and participate in local cultural experiences and wellness activities.
