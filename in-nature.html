Eligibility

Understand who can join our yoga retreats. Whether you are new to yoga or an experienced practitioner, our retreats are open to everyone looking to deepen their practice and explore a yogic lifestyle.

You can enroll in the retreat if you:

  •   Aged between 18 years to 75 years
  •   Children, families, students and/or backpacker
  •   No prior yoga asana or meditation experience is required
  •   Planning to spend some time in nature and connect to your inner self
