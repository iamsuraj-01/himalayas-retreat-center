About Us

At Himalayas Soul Retreat, we welcome you to experience a life-changing retreat in the spiritual heart of Rishikesh, India. Nestled along the serene banks of the Ganges River and surrounded by the majestic Himalayas, our center offers a chance to immerse yourself fully in yoga, meditation, and self-reflection. With the guidance of our seasoned instructors, you’ll discover new depths to your practice, experiencing profound physical and emotional benefits.

Whether you're new to yoga or an experienced practitioner, our tailored approach ensures that every retreat is personalized to meet your needs, helping you grow physically, mentally, and spiritually. Our expert instructors guide you through practices that blend traditional and modern techniques, ensuring a comprehensive and enriching experience. You’ll have the opportunity to deepen your understanding of yoga philosophy, connect with like-minded individuals, and explore mindfulness in a supportive environment. Beyond yoga, indulge in activities like nature walks, temple visits, and Ayurveda-inspired meals that complement your wellness journey.
